Leveraging the First Steps

Aims to give small business owners and women entrepreneurs an outlet to communicate with mentors, facilitators, and local government through many features. Each business owner, entrepreneur, facilitator, mentor, or government organization creates a profile that allow them to communicate with other people and network. With these profiles users can access many different people, their ideas, and advice.
